# house

This is intended to be a living document of the ideas I would like to see in a house. Many of these items were discovered or thought of after owning my first house, was well as seeing many of the houses my friends and family have lived in over the years.

Feel free to reference, reuse and redistribute this information as desired.

I am not recommending or endorsing any of the ideas below.
I am not responsible for the consequences of applying these ideas to your house.

---

## Core Goals
This house should:

1. Be as future-proof as possible
    1. baby/child/family friendly and as safe as possible for all stages of life, no after-build customization needed
        - should be able to fit a family of 5 with in-laws (i.e., +4), comfortably
2. Minimize:
    1. maintenance
    2. recurring cost
    3. bugs (ex., ultra clean garage, no cardboard or unused/unneeded items anywhere, all furniture comes off ground or has minimal contact with ground)
3. Maximize:
    1. beauty
4. as natural as possible. all natural materials where possible, nothing that is toxic
5. Not cut corners to save on cost or ease of implementation

- minimal maintenance/upkeep needed (ex. one story house, not 2, no fireplace, no handles on cabinets, doorstops on hinges)
- minimal monthly cost (ex. minimize energy consumption with things like manual mower (preferring manual labor over energy spend), solar, buy media, ...)
    - optimized energy cost
    - best possible insulation
    - humidity control to help prevent mold
    - 1 story house for less AC and maintenance cost


Note: to meet many of these goals, this would most likely need to be a new build

### House
#### General
- doorstops on hinges
- `1.1` farmhouse shutters (not blinds)

##### Flooring
- `4.0` Either:
    - natural, untreated, unfinished wood (i.e., something without chemicals) or
	- natural stone and [lime mortar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lime_mortar) for grout (i.e., tile)
        - for either option: aim for it to be completely flat (rather than regular tile that dips where the grout is) for easier cleaning.
- dedicated server room
    - with server rack
    - ultra air conditioned
- make them seal the grout?
- `5.0` plenty of ethernet plugs and power plugs on ground
- all electric (induction stovetop, no lng)

#### Kitchen
- fridge flush with or built into cabinets?
- `2.1` - no handles on cabinets

### Garage
- regular staircase to attic (like open a door, walk up stairs), not pull down ladder
- a/c / sealed garage? HVAC in garage? humidity control?
- 3 car garage or workshop?
- sink in garage
- drain on floor of garage
- vent hood in garage?
- epoxy or no?

### Yard
- Enough land that we have neighbors but they don't live 10 feet away. we want neighbors but we don't want them to be too close
    - likely 1-2 acres of land
    - not in a flood plane + drainage of yard has been verified (flood proof)
        - `2.2` thus no flood insurance needed
- `3.1` No vision blocking fences, if anything we have a picket-like fence or a fence like the following:
    - ["Open" Fence](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/0/02/Typical_suburban_backyard.jpg)
	- [or split rail](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Split-rail_fence)
- `5.0` rebar in driveway/concrete
    - No driveway at all? No concrete except for house slab? dirt driveway? More natural?
- bermuda grass
- house mostly shaded by trees? what about solar roof? large yard so house can be shaded and still have room for sun?
- natural landscaping not mowed yard?

### Miscellaneous
- `3.1` lots of plants / green
- `2.2` ag or religious exemption?

#### Power
- [Tesla Solar Roof](https://www.tesla.com/solarroof)
- `2.1` freeze proof house (i.e., nothing needs to be done if there is an extended freeze coming)
- `2.1` `2.2` House-wide [water softening](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Water_softening) + filtration system 
- screened in outdoor patio (w/ fridge + grill + sink built into counter? do these counteract `2.2` and `2.1`?)
- `4.0` no plastics or PVC for plumbing (if possible)
    - maybe copper?
- `2.2` vacuum sealed glass
- `2.2` vacuum insulated paneling
